Privacy Policy for Hexkeep

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ozcan Yesilyurt ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Hexkeep ("the Game"), a mobile match-3 RPG. By playing the Game you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

1. Who we are

The Game is developed and operated by Ozcan Yesilyurt, an independent developer. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at hexkeepgame@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or address. Your game progress is stored locally on your device. We and our service providers automatically collect limited technical and usage data, including:

  • Device and technical information , device model, operating system version, language, region, app version, and general (coarse) location inferred from IP address.
  • Gameplay and usage analytics , in-game events such as starting/finishing a run, levels reached, in-game currency earned or spent, and feature usage. This helps us understand and improve the Game.
  • Crash and diagnostic data , if the Game crashes or errors, we collect crash reports and device state (e.g. graphics driver, memory) to fix problems.
  • Advertising data , a mobile advertising identifier and ad-interaction data, used to show and measure ads (see Advertising below).
  • Purchase data , if you buy in-app items, the purchase is processed by the app store (Google Play). We receive a record that a purchase occurred; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

3. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Game and its features.
  • To diagnose crashes and technical problems.
  • To display and measure advertising.
  • To process and deliver in-app purchases.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

4. Service providers we use

We use trusted third-party services that process data on our behalf. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics) , usage analytics and crash reporting. Firebase privacy / Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob , in-app advertising. How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
  • Google Play Billing , in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information for money.

5. Advertising and your choices

The Game shows ads provided by Google AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, ads may be personalized (based on your advertising identifier) or non-personalized (based only on context).

  • EEA, UK, and Switzerland: the first time you play, we show a consent message (via Google's User Messaging Platform) asking whether we may use your data for personalized ads. You can change your choice at any time in Settings → Privacy → Manage inside the Game.
  • United States: you may opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information as described in the in-game privacy options and by your device controls.
  • You can also reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).

6. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on: your consent (for personalized ads and analytics where required); our legitimate interests (to keep the Game working, secure, and improving, and to show non-personalized ads); and performance of a contract (to deliver in-app purchases you request).

7. Children's privacy

Hexkeep is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13 (or under the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact hexkeepgame@gmail.com and we will delete it.

8. Data retention

Analytics and crash data are retained only as long as needed for the purposes above and according to our service providers' standard retention periods, after which they are deleted or anonymized. Your local game progress remains on your device until you delete the Game or clear its data.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or US state privacy laws), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, to object to certain processing, and to opt out of personalized advertising. Because we do not hold account-identifying information, please contact us at hexkeepgame@gmail.com to make a request, and use the in-game privacy options for advertising choices.

10. International transfers

Our service providers may process data on servers located outside your country, including the United States. Where required, they use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Security

We and our providers use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice in the Game.

13. Contact
