Privacy Policy for Hexkeep

Last updated: 17 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ozcan Yesilyurt ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Hexkeep ("the Game"), a mobile match-3 RPG. By playing the Game you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

1. Who we are

The Game is developed and operated by Ozcan Yesilyurt, an independent developer. For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at hexkeepgame@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or address. Your game progress is stored locally on your device and, to protect it against loss, is also backed up to our cloud save service (see the "Cloud saves" item below). We and our service providers automatically collect limited technical and usage data, including:

  • Device and technical information , device model, operating system version, language, region, app version, and general (coarse) location inferred from IP address.
  • Gameplay and usage analytics , in-game events such as starting/finishing a run, levels reached, in-game currency earned or spent, and feature usage. This helps us understand and improve the Game.
  • Crash and diagnostic data , if the Game crashes or errors, we collect crash reports and device state (e.g. graphics driver, memory) to fix problems.
  • Advertising data , a mobile advertising identifier and ad-interaction data, used to show and measure ads (see Advertising below).
  • An anonymous player identifier , a randomly generated ID created on your device the first time you play. It lets us link your crash reports to your gameplay events so we can reproduce a bug. It is not your name, email, or account, it is not tied to any real-world identity, and we never attempt to link it to one.
  • Purchase data , if you buy in-app items, the purchase is processed by the app store (the Apple App Store on iOS, Google Play on Android). We receive a record that a purchase occurred; we do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Cloud saves , your game progress (levels, currencies, items, quest progress, and purchase entitlements) is backed up to Google Firebase under an automatically created, anonymous account, so your save can be restored if you reinstall the Game or move to a new device. To make that restore possible, a randomly generated recovery credential is kept in your device's secure storage (on iOS: the Keychain and iCloud key-value storage; on Android: Google Play services). These identifiers exist solely for save recovery, they are never used for advertising, and they are not tied to your real-world identity.

3. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Game and its features.
  • To diagnose crashes and technical problems.
  • To display and measure advertising.
  • To process and deliver in-app purchases.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

4. Service providers we use

We use trusted third-party services that process data on our behalf. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Authentication & Cloud Firestore) , usage analytics, crash reporting, and cloud saves. Firebase privacy / Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob , in-app advertising. How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
  • Apple In-App Purchase (iOS) and Google Play Billing (Android) , in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy / Google Privacy Policy.
  • FreeIPAPI , converts your IP address into a general (coarse) location so the Game can match its day/night sky to your local time of day. We do not store the result on our servers, and we do not receive your precise location. freeipapi.com.

We do not sell your personal information for money.

5. Advertising and your choices

The Game shows ads provided by Google AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, ads may be personalized (based on your advertising identifier) or non-personalized (based only on context).

  • EEA, UK, and Switzerland: the first time you play, we show a consent message (via Google's User Messaging Platform) asking whether we may use your data for personalized ads. You can change your choice at any time in Settings → Privacy → Manage inside the Game.
  • iOS (App Tracking Transparency): on Apple devices the Game asks for your permission before it uses your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to track you across other companies' apps and websites. If you decline, you still see ads, but they are non-personalized. You can change your answer at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or turn off Allow Apps to Request to Track to decline for every app.
  • United States: you may opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information as described in the in-game privacy options and by your device controls.
  • You can also reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).

6. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on: your consent (for personalized ads and analytics where required); our legitimate interests (to keep the Game working, secure, and improving, and to show non-personalized ads); and performance of a contract (to deliver in-app purchases you request).

7. Children's privacy

Hexkeep is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under 13 (or under the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact hexkeepgame@gmail.com and we will delete it.

8. Data retention and deletion

Analytics and crash data are retained only as long as needed for the purposes above and according to our service providers' standard retention periods, after which they are deleted or anonymized. Your local game progress remains on your device until you delete the Game or clear its data. Your cloud save is retained so it can be restored after a reinstall, and is deleted on request: see our data deletion page for the steps.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or US state privacy laws), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, to object to certain processing, and to opt out of personalized advertising. Because we do not hold account-identifying information, please contact us at hexkeepgame@gmail.com to make a request (deletion steps are on our data deletion page), and use the in-game privacy options for advertising choices.

10. International transfers

Our service providers may process data on servers located outside your country, including the United States. Where required, they use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Security

We and our providers use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice in the Game.
